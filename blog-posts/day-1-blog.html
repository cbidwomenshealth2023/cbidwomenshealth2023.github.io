Day 1: Departure Day

  • Teja Sathi
  • August 6, 2022

Hello reader! Welcome to the CBID Women's Health team's travel blog. During the course of our global trip to Uganda, our team will be sharing updates on where our investigations take us.

Today our team is departing from Dulles Airport and finally embarking on our journey to Uganda! We've spent the past few weeks meeting virtually with some of our in-country partners and researching the women's health landscape in Uganda.

Our research has shed light on interesting initiatives and areas of potential clinical need. However, we all know that what happens in real life is rarely a perfect mirror of literature, and so, we're really excited to talk to more people on the ground and investigate these care pathways for ourselves!

We're really excited to have you with us, and hope you stick around to follow along our journey!
